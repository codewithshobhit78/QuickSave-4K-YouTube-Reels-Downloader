Link Shortener — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long will my short link work?

A: Short links are stored permanently unless removed manually. We use a small SQLite database — links remain across server restarts.

Q: Can I delete a short link?

A: Currently there is no self-serve delete. If you need a link removed, email support@quicksave4k.in with the short id and reason.

Q: Is the shortener private?

A: We store only the long URL and minimal server logs. Do not submit sensitive personal info in the URL itself.

Q: Why am I seeing "Invalid or expired short link"?

A: This can happen if the short id is mistyped or does not exist. If you just created the link and it fails, try refreshing — otherwise contact support.

Q: Can I use short links for ads or commercial campaigns?

A: Yes, but ensure the destination URL follows policies and is not used for spam or phishing. Abuse may cause removal.
