Privacy Policy — Link Shortener

This Privacy Policy explains how QuickSave 4K’s Link Shortener feature collects and uses information. This page applies only to the Link Shortener tool at quicksave4k.in/shorten.

What we collect

  • Original URL: We store the long URL you submit so we can redirect the short link to it.
  • Basic logs: Server logs (IP, user agent, timestamps) may be retained by the hosting provider for security and debugging.

How we use it

Submitted URLs are stored so the short link continues to redirect. We do not analyze or sell URLs. We may use aggregate, anonymized logs to diagnose issues and improve the service.

Retention

Short links are stored in our database until you request deletion or as described in our Terms. If you need a link removed, contact: support@quicksave4k.in.

Third parties

We use third-party services (hosting, analytics, AdSense). These providers may collect limited technical information; their privacy policies apply to their services.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@quicksave4k.in.
