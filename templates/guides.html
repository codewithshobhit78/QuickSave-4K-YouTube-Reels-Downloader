QuickSave4K — Guides & Tutorials

How to download a YouTube video

  1. Copy the YouTube video URL (desktop or mobile browser).
  2. Open QuickSave4K and paste the URL into the input box.
  3. Select MP4 (video) or MP3 (audio) and choose quality (1080p / 720p / etc).
  4. Click Download → wait for the progress bar → click the final download link.

How to download Instagram Reels

  1. Open the Reel on Instagram web or app, copy the shareable link.
  2. Paste the link into QuickSave4K and choose MP4.
  3. Note: Instagram may compress reels — highest available is often 1080p.

Troubleshooting tips

  • If downloads fail with 403, refresh cookies or try another video.
  • Check server logs for yt-dlp errors if backend issues appear.
  • Large video downloads are slow on shared hosts — prefer Railway with enough resources or run locally.
